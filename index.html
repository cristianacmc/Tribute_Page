VINT CERF

one of the father's of the internet

    Here's a time line of Vin Cerf's life:

  • 1994 - ACM Fellow
  • 1988 - Fellow of the IEEE, "for contributions and leadership in the design, development, and application of internet protocols"
  • 1994 - Association for Computing Machinery, for "vision and leadership in the design, implementation, evolution, and dissemination of the TCP/IP computer communication protocol suite"
  • 1996 - Yuri Rubinsky Memorial Award
  • 2008 - Cerf was inducted into the Worshipful Company of Information Technologists and given the Freedom of the City of London
  • 2008 - He and Robert Kahn were awarded the Japan Prize
  • 2011 - he was awarded an HPI Fellowship as "...a tribute to his work for a new medium which influenced the everyday life of our society like no other one
  • 2012 - he was inducted as a Pioneer into the Internet Hall of Fame"
  • 2013 - Cerf was one of five Internet and Web pioneers awarded the inaugural Queen Elizabeth Prize for Engineering
